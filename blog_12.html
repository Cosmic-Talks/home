Save Your Time With Artificial Intelligence

Published On : March 25th , 2023

Author : Tapas Pandey

With the onset of 21st century, Artificial Intelligence has surely impacted the lives of many of us. From morning weather update to letting this technology create the complex algorithms for us independently, AI can correctly be said as the time saviour of present day. Let's see some of the AI tools which you can use for free to enhance your productivity and save the time.

1.Chat GPT
Large-scale language model ChatGPT was created by OpenAI. It produces responses to text-based input that resemble those of a human using deep learning algorithms. Its goals are to enhance natural language processing abilities and give conversational support.

Try here

2. DALL-E
DALL-E is an artificial intelligence programme created by OpenAl that can produce visuals from textual descriptions. Based on verbal prompts, it generates a variety of images, including objects, animals, settings, and abstract notions, using a combination of deep learning techniques and generative models.

Try here

3. Lumen 5
Lumen5 turns text into interesting videos using AI. You just need to submit your content, and Lumen5's AI analyses it to make suggestions for images and videos. You can modify your videos just by choosing several themes, adding music, changing the voice, and changing the text.

Try here

4. Yippity
Yippity is an AI-powered productivity tool that converts your notes or internet data into powerful quizes & interactive flashcards to allow efficient study and enhanced understanding.

Try here

5. Tome
Tome is an AI powered platform designed to make the process of presenataion and design plans interactive and fun with automated story telling format. This tool can certainly help you in completing your tasks efficiently and that too before the deadlines !
